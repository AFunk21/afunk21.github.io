Audience

The audience of this site are people who need their local weather info for everyday activities. This will help them plan out thier day and be prepared for what is coming.

Personas

Persona 1: Mike loves biking, he usually bikes to work. He needs a weather update in order to see if he should bike to work or take his car.

Persona 2: Jen coaches a high school soccer team.

Senarios

  • Mike checks the weather for the week to see which days he can bike.
  • Jen checks for lightning to make sure her team can play safely.
  • On the weekend Mike takes 10 mile bikeride. He checks the weather to make sure it wont rain while he is riding.
  • Jen notices that it is lightly raining but that wont stop her team from practicing
